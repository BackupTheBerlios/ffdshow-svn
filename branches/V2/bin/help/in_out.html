FFDSHOW

Codecs

 

XVID

  XviD MPEG4 video codec (www.xvid.org)

MP43

  Microsoft MPEG-4 Video Codec V3 (used in some ASFs)

MP42

  Microsoft MPEG-4 Video Codec V2

MP41

  Microsoft MPEG-4 Video Codec V1 (old and low quality)

WMV1

  Windows Media Video 7

BLZ0

  Blizzard movies

Raw video

  ffdshow will be able to process YUY2 and RGB video on input which means it can provide prostprocessing for most of other video decoders

  

Decode using XviD

If xvid.dll is found, ffdshow can use it instead of libavcodec to decompress MPEG4 compatible movies (DIVX 4, XVID, partially DIVX 5).
